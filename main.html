Introduction
Uncharted is a fun and useful tool to help 20-somethings jumpstart their travel plans.
Travel planning can be an overwhelming and tedious process. We set out to build a tool that makes the planning process as fun and exciting as exploring new places in person. In our Contextual Inquiry, we found that most college-aged travelers do not use or rely on any guide books or websites. We designed Uncharted to help students in the initial planning phase, choosing a destination and jumpstarting the logistics. Uncharted allows you to browse through snapshots of destinations around the world, and view details about things to do and where to stay in these locations. Enjoy!
